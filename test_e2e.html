Manual E2E Test

  1. Open key-tools.html.
  2. Create ECDH P-256 and Schnorr key pairs.
  3. Open encrypt.html.
  4. Choose a PNG or JPG cover image and enter a message.
  5. Paste the receiver public ECDH key and sender private Schnorr key.
  6. Choose modulo generator auto/manual settings, then click Encrypt & Sign and download the stego PNG.
  7. Open decrypt.html.
  8. Load the stego PNG, receiver private ECDH key, and trusted sender public Schnorr key.
  9. Click Decrypt & Verify.
  10. Confirm plaintext, AES-GCM decryption, signature validity, fingerprint match, modulo generator P/g, and LCG S1/S2 details.
